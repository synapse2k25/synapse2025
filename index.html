About GVMC

Government Vellore Medical College (GVMC), located in Adukkamparai, Tamil Nadu, is one of the state's premier medical institutions. Established with the mission to provide excellent medical education and healthcare, GVMC has consistently produced skilled and compassionate doctors. With its dedicated faculty, state-of-the-art facilities, and a strong emphasis on community service, GVMC stands as a beacon of medical excellence in the region.

About Synapse

Synapse is the annual intercollegiate cultural and sports fest organized by GVMC. It’s a platform where creativity, talent, and competitive spirit come alive. Students from various institutions gather to showcase their best – from thrilling sportsmanship to dazzling performances. Synapse celebrates unity, expression, and energy, making it one of the most awaited fests in the region.

General Instructions

  • ✔ Only MBBS, BDS and AYUSH students are allowed to participate (No cross-course teams).
  • ✔ Cultural events are open for all medical & dental colleges.
  • ✔ Registration is mandatory for all events.
  • ✔ Online registration should be completed before the deadline.
  • ✔ Limited slots available; priority will be given on a first-come-first-serve basis.
  • ✔ Registration fee is non-refundable and proof of payment (soft/hard copy) must be carried.
  • ✔ Participants must bring their College ID card and 2 passport-size photographs
  • ✔ SYNAPSE’25 delegate pass must be shown to the event organizers before the event.
  • ✔ Cultural participants must report at least 1 hour prior to the event at the venue.
  • ✔ Sports teams must report 30 minutes before the scheduled time or face disqualification.
  • ✔ Misbehavior or indiscipline can lead to denial of participation or disqualification.
  • ✔ Smoking, alcohol, and narcotics are strictly prohibited inside the college premises.
  • ✔ Any damage to college property will be dealt with severely.
  • ✔ Standard rules for respective games will be followed.
  • ✔ Teams may have more than one match on the same day; fixtures are subject to change.
  • ✔ Participants in multiple events must inform organizers in advance to avoid clashes.
  • ✔ Accommodation will be provided for participants who inform the in-charges in advance.
  • ✔ Transport to venues will be arranged and details will be shared with teams priorly.
  • ✔ Decisions of the judges, referees, and organizing committee will be final.
